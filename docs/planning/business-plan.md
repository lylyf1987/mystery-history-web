# Mystery History Website - Business Plan

## Overview
A website dedicated to sharing mystery content, unknown mysteries, and lesser-known real history stories with daily updates.

## Budget
- Initial funds: ~$500
- Estimated Year 1 cost: $15-20 (domain only)
- Hosting: Free (Netlify/Vercel)
- Tech stack: Static site (Hugo/Jekyll)

## Phases
1. Planning & Strategy
2. Development (Week 1-2)
3. Deployment (Week 2)
4. Content Workflow (Ongoing)
5. Monetization (After 3-6 months)
6. Monitoring

## Revenue Goals
- Short-term: Cover operational costs
- Long-term: Generate profit through AdSense, affiliates, donations
