About Mystery History

Mystery History is an educational history publication focused on historical mysteries, unusual events, and unresolved questions from reliable records and research literature.

What We Publish

  • Explainers about debated historical events and artifacts.
  • Context-driven summaries of newly reported discoveries.
  • Evidence-based comparisons of competing theories.

Editorial Team

Articles are written and reviewed by the Mystery History Editorial Team. We aim for clear language, explicit sourcing, and transparent uncertainty where evidence is incomplete.

Research Standards

  • We prioritize primary records, museums, academic publishers, and official government/archival sources.
  • We include references and further reading on each article page.
  • We distinguish between documented facts, interpretations, and open questions.

Corrections and Updates

If we identify a factual issue, we update the article promptly. See our Editorial Policy for full methodology and corrections process.
