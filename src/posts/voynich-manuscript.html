The Voynich Manuscript: The Book No One Can Read!

Published: February 20, 2026 • 7 min read

By Mystery History Editorial Team • Reviewed: March 4, 2026

A page from the mysterious Voynich Manuscript - can anyone crack its code?

Imagine finding a book that's 600 years old, filled with strange pictures of plants that don't exist, and written in a language no one on Earth can understand. That's the Voynich Manuscript - the most mysterious book in the world!

Even the world's smartest code-breakers have tried to read it. Scientists have used super-powerful computers. But after 100 years of trying, nobody has figured out what it says!

How Was It Found?

In 1912, a man named Wilfrid Voynich was looking through an old library in Italy when he found a strange book. It had 240 pages made of animal skin (called vellum), covered in weird curly writing that didn't look like any language he'd ever seen.

Even stranger were the pictures. They showed plants that didn't exist in real life - like someone had taken parts from different plants and glued them together!

📜 Fun Fact!

Scientists tested the book's pages and discovered they were made sometime between 1404 and 1438 - over 600 years ago! That's older than Christopher Columbus sailing to America!

The Weird Plants Inside

These plants don't match any real flowers on Earth - are they from someone's imagination?

One section of the book is filled with drawings of plants. But here's the weird part: many of the plants are impossible! They might have the roots of one plant, the leaves of another, and flowers from something completely different.

Some people think these might be made-up plants. Others wonder if the person who drew them was trying to keep their knowledge secret by mixing up the pictures on purpose.

The Strangest Pictures of All

But the weirdest part? The book has pages showing naked people bathing in green liquid, connected by tubes and pipes! It looks like some kind of strange science experiment from hundreds of years ago.

What are these people doing in the green pools? Nobody knows!

There are also drawings of stars and planets, but they don't match any patterns we see in our real night sky. It's almost like the book comes from a different world!

Who Wrote This Mystery Book?

Nobody knows for sure! Here are some guesses:

  • 🧙‍♂️ A medieval magician trying to hide secret knowledge
  • 💊 A doctor keeping recipes for mysterious medicines
  • 🎭 A prankster who wanted to fool people forever
  • 👽 Someone from a completely different time or place

🔍 The Code That Can't Be Cracked

The writing looks real - it has patterns like a real language. Words repeat, sentences flow, and there are even corrections! But no language on Earth matches it.

Computers can't decode it. Experts can't read it. Could YOU be the one to solve it?

See It For Yourself!

The real Voynich Manuscript is kept safe at Yale University in America. But you can see every page online for free! Just search for "Voynich Manuscript digital" and explore the mystery yourself.
