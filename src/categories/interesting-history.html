The Great Molasses Flood

In 1919, a massive tank of molasses exploded in Boston, sending a deadly wave of sticky destruction through the city. This bizarre disaster really happened!

The Cadaver Synod

In 897 AD, a dead pope was put on trial! His corpse was dug up, dressed in robes, and made to answer charges. One of history's strangest events!
